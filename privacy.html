Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Peace Time Agency, we prioritize the privacy of our creators. This policy outlines how we handle data via the TikTok API.

1. Data Collection via TikTok API

With your explicit consent, we access the following via TikTok API Services:

  • Profile Data: Username and avatar to personalize your dashboard.
  • Stats: Follower counts and like totals to track growth.
  • Content: A list of your public videos for performance analysis.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to provide analytics within the Peace Time Agency portal. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Deletion

Users can request data deletion at any time by emailing support@peacetimeagency.com. We will process all deletion requests within 48 hours.
