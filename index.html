Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Peace Time Agency, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data accessed via TikTok API Services to provide your agency dashboard and analytics.

TikTok API Compliance: By using our services, you agree to be bound by the TikTok Terms of Service and acknowledge the TikTok Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Access

With your explicit authorization, we collect the following data via TikTok API Scopes:

  • user.info.profile: Your display name and profile image to personalize your dashboard.
  • user.info.stats: Your follower, following, and like counts to track growth.
  • video.list: Metadata of your public videos to analyze performance and engagement.

2. Use of Data

We use this information exclusively to provide data visualization and growth insights. We never sell your data to third parties or use it for any purpose outside of the Peace Time Agency platform.

3. Data Revocation & Deletion

You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok app settings (Settings > Security > Manage App Permissions). To request the permanent deletion of your stored profile data from our servers, please contact us below.
