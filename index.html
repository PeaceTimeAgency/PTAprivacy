Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Peace Time Agency ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you connect your TikTok account to our services via the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Peace Time Agency through TikTok, we access the following data points:

  • Network Profile: We retrieve your TikTok display name, handle, and profile image to personalize your agency dashboard.
  • Account Statistics: We access follower counts, following counts, and total like counts to generate performance reports.
  • Video Catalog: We pull a list of your public video metadata to analyze engagement rates and content trends.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized growth analytics and reporting.
  • To improve our internal agency tools and creator services.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your TikTok data to any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your TikTok data only as long as your account is connected to our service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok app settings. To request a permanent deletion of all data stored on our servers, please contact us.
